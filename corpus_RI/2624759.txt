The '''discography''' of LeToya, an American R&B singer-songwriter  consists of two studio albums.
On her solo self-titled debut album ''Letoya''  she worked with producers Dave Young, Scott Storch, Jermaine Dupri, Bryan Michael Cox, Just Blaze, Johnta Austin, Teddy Bishop, JR Rotem and Mike City. As well as artists Paul Wall, Slim Thug, Mike Jones and Rick Ross. On her second album ''Lady Love'' she worked with producers Bei Maejor, Cory Bold, Tank, Ron "NEFF-U" Feemstar, Warren    Oak    Felder, Jerry "Texx" Franklin, Da Internz and Bridge. In addition to artists Ludacris, Estelle and Mims.
