Jerry now runs his own Web site, JerryBonkowski.com, having been laid-off at the end of 2008 from his position as National NASCAR Columnist for Yahoo! Sports. Even though he is no longer with Yahoo, he remains a part-time on-air personality on Sirius NASCAR Radio, Channel 128. Jerry also joined AOL Sports/FanHouse.com over a year ago as an editor. He has recently begun NFL coverage for FanHouse.com, as well, a return to his roots and the several years he previously covered the NFL for USA Today and other employers.
