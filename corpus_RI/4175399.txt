WAHS (89.5 FM, "The Station for Alteration") is a radio station broadcasting a variety format. Licensed to Auburn Hills, Michigan, it first began broadcasting in November 1975. The Station Director is Rick Kreinbring is passing the duties onto Aaron Donaghy. The station runs as both a public station and a learning tool for young students attending Avondale High School.
The station continues to rebroadcast WPHS in Warren 24/7 until students can begin running the station which is expected in January 2011.
