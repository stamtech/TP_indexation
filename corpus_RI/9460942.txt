To date (15th April 2011) six teams have confirmed to the Volvo Ocean Race that they will definitely be on the start line. However, at this time only five of the six teams are public and there are said to be over 10 more syndicates that are actively working on entering a team. One of the teams yet to declare is rumoured to be a Spanish entry which will be skippered by Iker Martinez. An Italian entry skippered by Giovanni Soldini has declared but does not feature on the Official website. It is understood that the likes of Bouwe Bekking, Matthew Humphries and former winner Mike Sanderson are working on new syndicates.
