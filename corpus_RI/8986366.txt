The '''Anaheim Regional Transportation Intermodal Center''' ('''ARTIC''') is a proposed transportation hub in the city of Anaheim, California. The project will be built on land owned by the City of Anaheim and the Orange County Transportation Authority, would serve to connect Orange County's bus network, the Southern California Metrolink rail system, and the planned California High-Speed Rail line in the city's Platinum Triangle.
In May, 2009 the architectural team of Parsons Brinckerhoff/HOK was selected for architectural/engineering design of ARTIC. As of Spring 2011, ARTIC was in the design development phase.
The station is scheduled to be operational by the year 2014.
