At the 2005 World Series of Poker (WSOP), Josephy won a bracelet in the $1,500 seven-card stud event, defeating Kirill Gerasimov in the final heads-up confrontation. He has also finished in the money in several World Poker Tour (WPT) events.
Cliff Josephy has earned a level of success in live events that his real name is fairly well known, but he is still known by the screen name "JohnnyBax" on the Internet. At different times he has been the number one ranked online poker tournament player on PocketFives.com. 
Josephy also works as a poker instructor.  Together with Eric Haber he has produced numerous training videos featuring professional players as they play poker online.
As of 2009, his total live tournament winnings exceed $1,500,000.
