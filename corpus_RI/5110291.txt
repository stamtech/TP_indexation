The '''Haier Black Pearl''' is a cell phone that is perhaps the world's smallest MP3 cell phone. It is manufactured by Haier. It has been described as "a lipstick case sized luxury phone" . Its a full featured phone with a host of features. Features include MP3, FM Stereo, BlueTooth, Voice Recorder and full color display.
