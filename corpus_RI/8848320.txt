 She was chair of the Local Government and Metropolitan Affairs Committee during the 2007-2008 biennium, and served as an assistant minority leader during the 2003-2004 biennium. On November 16, 2010, incoming Minority Leader Paul Thissen announced that she will be Deputy Minority Leader during the 2011-2012 legislative session.
Hillstrom is a life-long resident of her district. She graduated from Park Center Senior High School in Brooklyn Park, then went on to the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis, earning her B.A. in Sociology. She is currently a student at William Mitchell College of Law in Saint Paul. She served on the Brooklyn Center Planning Commission and was an advocate for the Brooklyn Center Domestic Abuse Intervention Program. She was a member of the Brooklyn Center City Council from 1995-2000.
