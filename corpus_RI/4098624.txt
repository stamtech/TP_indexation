Ann Warren was born on December 10, 1945 in Northampton, Massachusetts to printer Richard Warren and artist Marian Gray Warren. The second of three children, she and her brothers grew up in Williamsburg, Massachusetts. She earned a bachelor's degree at Bates College in Maine and a Master of Arts in Teaching at the University of Massachusetts.
Ann married Rick Turner on June 3, 1967. They have two children, Benjamin and Charlotte.
