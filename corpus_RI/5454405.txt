Parke attended Vanderbilt University in Nashville, where he received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics and Psychology.  He received a J.D. degree from Southern Methodist University in University Park, Texas. He began his legal career with Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld in Dallas, Texas. He later became a corporate partner with the law firm Hopkins & Sutter.
Parke became the executive vice president of Broadcast.com.  That company was acquired by Yahoo! in 1999.  Following that merger, Parke became vice-president and general manager of Yahoo! Broadcast.
In addition to his role with the Todd Wagner Foundation and KIPP Truth Academy, Parke is executive vice president of the Wagner/Cuban Companies. Parke is also president of Landmark Theatres, which currently operates 58 theatres in 23 markets.
