They are most famous for creating the 2 seat open top Indigo 3000 car. This car is powered by a Volvo 3&nbsp;Litre six cylinder engine giving a top speed of 250&nbsp;km/h (155&nbsp;mph) with drive to the rear wheels. The body is of composite panels attached to a space frame. The front suspension is a walking beam design with a transverse composite leaf spring and the rear unit is a multilink from Volvo also using a composite transverse spring.
