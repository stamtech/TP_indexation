The first tournament to reach a million dollar prize pool was the 1983 WSOP Main Event. The WSOP Main Event of 2003 had the first prize pool of above $10,000,000.
The largest non Hold'em Tournament has been the 2008 WSOP $50K HORSE with a prize pool of $7,104,000 and the first prize of $1,989,120 going to Scotty Nguyen.
