In 2008, he won the World Poker Tour Borgata Poker Open Main Event, securing a cash prize of $1,424,500.  He defeated a final table that included tournament veterans Dan Heimiller and Mark Seif to win the title.
Rajkumar has also had notable success at the LA Poker Classic tournaments. In 2008 he placed 1st out of 167 entrants in the $2,500 No Limit Hold'em for a prize of $113,425.
As of 2010, Rajkumar has total tournament winnings in excess of $3,582,000.
