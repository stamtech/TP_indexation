He qualified for the 2001 Thailand Masters. In 2006/2007 he reached the last 32 of two events, beating Mark Davis in both. He qualified for the 2007 Shanghai Masters by beating Mark King, but lost 5   3 to wildcard Yu Delu. He was one frame away from reaching the 2008 Welsh Open.
He is also involved in promotions work for World Snooker, and was in the presentation party at the final of the 2007 UK Championship
