The '''Mandalay Bay Convention Center''', located in Paradise, Nevada is one of the largest privately owned and operated convention centers in the world.  The  facility is owned by MGM Mirage.  It is attached to the Mandalay Bay Resort and Casino and adjacent to the Mandalay Bay Events Center.  The facility can also support up to 75 breakout sessions and has several ballrooms with the largest being .
At opening in January 2003, it was the 5th largest convention center in the United States.
