Prior to his current appointment, Dr. Epperly served as Director of the Protestant Ministry and Adjunct Professor in Theology, Spirituality, and Medicine at Georgetown University.Brussat, Frederic and Mary Ann Brussat. "Bruce Epperly."
His professional interests are broad and include process theology, constructive post-modernism, spirituality, healing, and ministerial transformation. He is noted for innovative work in joining theological reflection with spiritual formation, ministerial leadership, and healing ministry.,,
He is the author, or co-author, of sixteen books, including ''Tending to the Holy: The Practice of the Presence of God in Ministry'', the Academy of Parish Clergy's 2009 Book of the Year, and ''Holy Adventure: 41 Days of Audacious Living'', his response to Rick Warren   s ''Purpose Driven Life''.
