Following is a list of all '''United States federal judges appointed by President Barack Obama''' during his presidency. 
As of June 14, 2011, 88 Obama nominees to Article III judgeships have been confirmed by the United States Senate, namely two Justices to the Supreme Court of the United States, nineteen judges to the United States courts of appeals, and 67 judges to the United States district courts. 50 further nominations are currently awaiting Senate action., ''uscourts.gov'' There are currently 16 vacancies on the United States Courts of Appeals, 71 vacancies on the United States district courts and 19 additional federal judiciary vacancies that are anticipated to occur before the end of Obama's current term., ''uscourts.gov'' Obama has not made any recess appointments to the federal courts. 
Obama also designated sitting judge Emily C. Hewitt of the United States Court of Federal Claims, an Article I court, to be Chief Judge of that court.
