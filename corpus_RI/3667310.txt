Originally the Washington State Pavilion of Expo '74, '''Spokane Center''' is a facility located on the south bank of the Spokane River in Downtown Spokane, Washington.  It consists of the  Spokane Convention Center, and the INB Performing Arts Center.  Spokane Center is owned and operated by the Spokane Public Facilities District (SPFD).
