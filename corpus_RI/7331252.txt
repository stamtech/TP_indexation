Assemblyman Miller graduated from Foothill High School. He received his bachelors degree from Cal State Fullerton in 1985.
Assemblyman Miller was elected to the Corona, CA City Council in 2000. He was also the leader of the 91 Advisory Committee made up of elected officials from both Riverside and Orange counties. Furthermore, Miller served as a delegate to the Republican National Convention in 2004, and was elected the next year to the position of Special Elections Chair of the Riverside County Republican Party. He was elected Riverside County Republican Party chairman in 2006.
