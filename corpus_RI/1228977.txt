The station consists of an island platform located in the center median of State Route 24. The station is located on the east side of the Caldecott Tunnel
Service at this station began on May 21, 1973. BART website, access date July 13, 2008
This station is in BART District 1 and is represented by Gail Murray who is also current BART President., BART.gov, retrieved January 23, 2008
The solar radiation is the best at this station in the entire system and it has therefore been earmarked for the installation of car ports with solar panels on top of them in 2008., by Dennis Cuff, ''Contra Costa Times'', July 10, 2008, access date July 13, 2008
