Toussaint was appointed Chief Judge in 1995 by Minnesota Governor Arne Carlson. He held the position until October 2010, when he stepped down as Chief Judge to take a seat on the court as an associate judge. He said he intends to remain on the court until October 2011, when he will reach the state's mandatory retirement age.
Toussaint also serves as an Adjunct Professor of Law at both William Mitchell College of Law in Saint Paul and the University of St. Thomas School of Law in Minneapolis. Prior to his service on the Court of Appeals, he served as a district judge in Hennepin County.
