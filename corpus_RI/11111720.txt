Riley grew up in Austin, Texas and received his bachelor's degree in Economics with Majors in Finance & Entrepreneurial Management from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
Riley joined Yahoo! in January 1999 when Yahoo! acquired Log-Me-On.com, the start-up company he co-founded, which developed and patented what is today the Yahoo! Toolbar.  In 2008, Riley was promoted to Senior Vice President and Managing Director of EMEA, and reports to Carol Bartz, CEO of Yahoo!
He began his career as a financial analyst at Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette in New York City.
Riley lives in Switzerland with his wife and their four children. 
He serves on the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania Entrepreneurial Advisory Board, the Board of Swiss-American Chamber of Commerce and is a Member of the Young Presidents' Organization(YPO).
