The '''Hoya de Los Vicentes Solar Plant''' () is a photovoltaic power station in Jumilla, Murcia in Spain. The solar park covers area of some  and comprises a group of 200&nbsp;photovoltaic arrays with a total capacity of 23&nbsp;MW.  A total of 120,000&nbsp;solar panels have been installed in the facility.. The project generates energy equivalent to the annual consumption of 20,000&nbsp;households.
