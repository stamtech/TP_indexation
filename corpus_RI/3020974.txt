The American edition began as a bimonthly in October 2004 and went monthly in August 2005.
In December 2006, Bluff Magazine purchased thepokerdb.com, an online tournament database.  The site has since been integrated into the Bluff Magazine web site.
Printed in the United Kingdom and focusing more on the European poker circuit, regular contributors include professional players including Neil Channing, Liv Boeree, Tom Sambrook, Phil Laak, Antonio Esfandiari, Dr. Les Golden, and Mike Caro.
The magazine was first published in March 2006.
Published by Maverick Publishing Corp., Bluff Magazine SA focuses mainly on the poker industry in Southern Africa. Ryan Dreyer, top South African poker player and winner of the 2008 Sun City Millions poker tournament, is the editor.
